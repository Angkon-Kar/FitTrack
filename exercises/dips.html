Dips

Bench or parallel bar dips. One of the best compound push movements for mass and strength.

TricepsLower ChestFront DeltoidsCore
Category Push
Difficulty 🟡 Intermediate
Primary Triceps, Lower Chest
Sets · Reps 3 · 5–8 bench
📋 Step-by-Step Form
1
Setup
Bench: hands on edge behind you, legs extended. Bars: grip parallel bars, arms extended overhead.
2
Controlled Descent
Lower body by bending elbows. Lean forward for chest, stay upright for triceps focus.
3
Depth
Lower until elbows reach 90°. Do not exceed 90° until very experienced.
4
Drive Up
Press through palms, fully extend arms at top. Exhale on the way up.

⚠️ Common Mistakes
✗Going below 90° — dangerous for shoulder capsule
✗Shrugging shoulders up — depress them throughout
✗Elbows flaring too wide
✅ Pro Tips
→Bench dips easier; parallel bars harder and more effective
→Lean forward slightly for more chest activation
→Add weight via backpack for progressive overload
