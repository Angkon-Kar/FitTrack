Pistol Squat

Single-leg squat to full depth. The pinnacle of lower body calisthenics strength.

QuadricepsGlutesHamstringsCoreAnkle Stabilizers
Category Legs
Difficulty 💀 Elite
Primary Quadriceps, Glutes
Sets · Reps 3 · 3–5/leg
📋 Step-by-Step Form
1
Prerequisites
Excellent ankle mobility, strong quads, single-leg balance. Practice assisted version first.
2
Stand on One Leg
Extend other leg in front. Arms forward for balance. Core tight.
3
Controlled Descent
Slowly lower on one leg. Keep heel planted. Extended leg parallel to floor.
4
Full Depth
Sit all the way down — hamstring touching calf. Exceptional mobility required.
5
Drive Back Up
Push through heel. Quad drives movement. Stand fully upright to complete.

⚠️ Common Mistakes
✗Heel lifting — work on ankle dorsiflexion daily
✗Excessive forward lean — mobility work needed
✗Using momentum — true pistol is slow and controlled
✅ Pro Tips
→Progression: TRX/ring hold → Box pistol → Assisted → Full
→Ankle stretches daily accelerate progress significantly
→Box pistol to a low surface is an excellent intermediate step
