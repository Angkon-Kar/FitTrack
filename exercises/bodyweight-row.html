Bodyweight Row

Australian Pull-Up / Inverted Row. The best horizontal pull and perfect pull-up preparation.

Latissimus DorsiRhomboidsBicepsCoreRear Deltoids
Category Pull
Difficulty 🟢 Beginner
Primary Latissimus Dorsi, Rhomboids
Sets · Reps 3 · 8–12
📋 Step-by-Step Form
1
Setup
Use a low bar, table edge, or rings at waist height. Lie underneath, grip bar, heels on floor, body straight.
2
Starting Position
Arms extended, body rigid like a plank facing UP. Lower bar = harder.
3
Pull Chest to Bar
Pull chest to bar, leading with chest not chin. Squeeze shoulder blades together at top.
4
Slow Descent
Lower with full arm extension at bottom. That is one rep.

⚠️ Common Mistakes
✗Hips sagging — keep body straight like a plank
✗Not pulling shoulder blades together at top
✗Bar too high — makes it too easy to be effective
✅ Pro Tips
→Great if you cannot do pull-ups yet
→Elevate feet to increase difficulty significantly
→Add a pause at top for extra muscle activation
