Calf Raises

Often neglected but crucial for explosive performance. Calves need very high volume to grow.

GastrocnemiusSoleusTibialis Anterior
Category Legs
Difficulty 🟢 Beginner
Primary Gastrocnemius, Soleus
Sets · Reps 3 · 15–20
📋 Step-by-Step Form
1
Position
Stand on a step edge with heels hanging off, or flat on floor. Hold wall for balance.
2
Full Range Down
Let heels drop BELOW step level. This full stretch is the most important part.
3
Rise on Toes
Push up onto balls of feet as high as possible. Hold peak contraction 1 second.
4
Controlled Descent
Slowly lower heels back down. Full stretch at bottom. One rep complete.
🌬️ Breathing Pattern
Going Down
Inhale down
At Bottom
Pause
Coming Up
Exhale up

⚠️ Common Mistakes
✗Partial range — must have full stretch AND full contraction
✗Bouncing — slow and controlled wins every time
✗Skipping the 1-second peak hold at the top
✅ Pro Tips
→Calves respond to HIGH volume — target 100+ reps weekly
→Single-leg raises double intensity immediately
→Step-edge stretch is non-negotiable for full development
